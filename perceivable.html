Captions

Captions are provided for all prerecorded audio content in synchronized media, except when the media is a media alternative for text and is clearly labeled as such.

While, as developers, we cannot ensure captions exist, we can do 2 things.

  1. Say something if you find yourself working on non-captioned material.
  2. Make sure you know how to add captions.
